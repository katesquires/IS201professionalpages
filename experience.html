Missionary Service in Peru and Utah

During my missionary service for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I gained valuable experience in leadership and teamwork. Starting with a small congregation of six attendees, I worked to inspire and unify individuals, ultimately growing the congregation to nearly 100 members. I also mentored over 20 Sister Missionaries, helping them find passion and enthusiasm for their work. This experience taught me how to connect with others, lead with empathy, and create meaningful impact within a community.

Humanitarian Projects in Mexico

My volunteer work in Mexico allowed me to contribute to meaningful construction projects that improved the lives of underprivileged children. I helped build a preschool over 2 ½ weeks and assisted in renovating an orphanage. These efforts gave children access to education and better living conditions, showing me the importance of teamwork, perseverance, and giving back to the community. These experiences remain some of the most rewarding of my life.

Freelance Photography Business

As a freelance photographer, I’ve conducted over 50 client sessions specializing in portrait, family, and wedding photography. From client consultations to advanced photo editing with Adobe Lightroom, I managed all aspects of the business, delivering high-quality images that exceeded expectations. My ability to consistently meet client needs built a loyal customer base, leading to repeat business and referrals. This experience strengthened my entrepreneurial mindset and attention to detail.
